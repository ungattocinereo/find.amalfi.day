Quick Summary

Walk from Amalfi through the coastal tunnel to Atrani. The meeting point is just past the tunnel exit at the Atrani center.

Where are you coming from?

From Amalfi ~12 min walk · 15 steps

From Atrani

The meeting point is right at the tunnel exit in Atrani. If you're already in Atrani, it's just a short walk.

At the tunnel exit, you'll see the road leading into Atrani with directional signs ahead.

This is the meeting point. You've arrived!
